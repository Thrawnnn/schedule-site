Who Are We?

We are a Citizenship development program.

We are sponsored by the Navy, but we are not a Military program. We do not require service after High School.

The NJROTC mission is to instill in students in United States secondary educational institutions the values of citizenship, service to the United States, personal responsibility, and a sense of accomplishment.

NOTICE: This is a Passion Project website for my Unit, this is not officially sponsored by the SVHS NJROTC program!
